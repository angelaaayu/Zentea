CATEGORIES OF TEA

Every category is a voyage into unique flavors and traditions. Explore our carefully curated selection, ranging from timeless classics to contemporary blends, and embark on a delightful journey through the world of tea.

Black Tea

Indulge in the bold and invigorating essence of black tea. With its rich and robust flavor, our collection of premium black teas offers a comforting and energizing experience with every sip. From classic blends to unique infusions, explore the timeless allure of black tea.

Oolong Tea

Embark on a journey of nuanced taste with Oolong tea. Our curated selection of Oolong teas presents a harmonious balance between the complexities of green and black teas. Delight in the captivating aroma and semi-oxidized character of Oolong, a true testament to the artistry of tea crafting.

White Tea

Experience the delicate elegance of white tea. Gently plucked and minimally processed, our white tea collection captures the essence of purity and subtlety. With its light and refreshing character, white tea offers a soothing escape and a taste of nature's tranquility in every cup.

Matcha

Elevate your senses with the vibrant and ceremonial Matcha experience. Crafted from shade-grown tea leaves, our Matcha selection invites you to savor a moment of focused calm. Indulge in its brilliant color, rich umami taste, and the invigorating energy that only true Matcha can provide.

GALLERY

Step into our immersive tea gallery, a haven for tea enthusiasts seeking to indulge in the art of flavor. Each tea showcased here tells a tale of craftsmanship, offering a visual and sensory feast that invites you to explore the rich tapestry of aromas, hues, and tastes.
